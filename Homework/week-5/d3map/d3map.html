The mouseover effects in my figure are highlighting the country and generatign a pop up with the name of the country and the specific population number. I did this because my figure is a map filled in with colors based on the popualtion number and this mouseover effect enables the user to see exactly which country he is seeing on the map and what the name and popualtion is of that country. This interactivity makes the map more specific than a global map colored in. My choice for color is a sequential choice. It has the color purple from dark to light, which gives a good contrast between high population (dark) and a low population (light). The countries with unknown data are colored yellow, so they are easily visible between the known data points.
